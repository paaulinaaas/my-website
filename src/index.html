About Me

Hello! My name is Paulina and I'm a Frontend Developer.
I work with modern technologies like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and ES6. At this moment I am focused on learning React too! I also use a wide range of web tools such as Git, Gulp, or Webpack. All these things help me to achieve great results and make my code well-planned, browser-compatibile and clean.

I'm particularly interested in accessibility, performance optimization and web animations, but I enjoy every topic that helps me to achieve the best results. I'm fast and efficient learner, attentive to detail, and focused on writing consistent and well-structured code that is easy to maintain.

I'm also very enthusiastic and friendly person with a lot of positive energy and smile on the face. I'm trying to be creative at my work, and look outside of the box. I can definitely say that frontend is my passion :)
